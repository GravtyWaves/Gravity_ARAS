# ARAS Microservice Team Prompt Document

This prompt document is designed for use by AI assistants and project managers to assemble, manage, and optimize a multidisciplinary team for the ARAS microservice. Each role is defined with clear responsibilities, ensuring the team covers all technical and domain expertise required for a world-class news analysis platform.

---

## Team Structure & Roles

### 1. Technical Team

- **Software Architect**
  - Design system architecture, integration, scalability, and security.
- **Backend Developers (Python, Django/FastAPI)**
  - Build APIs, business logic, and integrate NLP/analytics modules.
- **Frontend Developers (React, D3.js, Chart.js)**
  - Develop dashboards, user interfaces, and data visualizations.
- **DevOps Engineer**
  - Manage CI/CD, deployment, server infrastructure, and monitoring.
- **Database Engineer**
  - Design schemas, optimize queries, and manage migrations.
- **Data Scientist / NLP Engineer**
  - Develop models for entity extraction, sentiment, topic modeling, and trend detection.
- **Security Specialist**
  - Implement authentication, encryption, and conduct security audits.
- **QA Engineer / Tester**
  - Ensure reliability, performance, and run automated/manual tests.

### 2. Domain Experts

- **Senior Journalist / News Analyst**
  - Select sources, validate content, and define news relevance.
- **Economist**
  - Advise on economic indicators, validate financial news and trends.
- **Political Analyst**
  - Provide expertise on political events, entities, and network analysis.
- **Sociologist**
  - Advise on social trends, sentiment, and impact analysis.
- **Cultural Expert**
  - Guide coverage of arts, culture, and related topics.
- **Sports Analyst**
  - Support sports news processing and trend identification.
- **Science & Technology Expert**
  - Validate tech/science news and assist in topic modeling.

### 3. Support & Management

- **Project Manager**
  - Coordinate activities, timelines, and communication.
- **Product Owner**
  - Define product vision, requirements, and priorities.
- **UI/UX Designer**
  - Design user flows, wireframes, and visual assets.
- **Documentation Specialist**
  - Prepare technical and user documentation.
- **Legal Advisor**
  - Ensure compliance with privacy and copyright laws.

---

## Team Member Qualifications

All team members must possess the following qualifications:

- **PhD or Doctorate in Relevant Field:**
  - Each technical and domain expert must hold a doctoral degree in their area of expertise (e.g., Computer Science, Data Science, Economics, Political Science, Sociology, etc.).

- **High IQ and Analytical Skills:**
  - Members should demonstrate exceptional intelligence, problem-solving ability, and analytical thinking.

- **Proven Expertise:**
  - Documented experience and advanced skills directly related to their role and responsibilities in the ARAS microservice project.

- **Specialized Knowledge:**
  - Deep understanding of the technologies, methodologies, and domain-specific requirements necessary for precise and complete project execution.

- **Collaboration and Leadership:**
  - Ability to work in multidisciplinary teams, communicate effectively, and lead initiatives when required.

---

This ensures the ARAS microservice team is composed of top-tier experts capable of delivering the highest quality results.

## Prompt Usage Instructions (for AI Assistants)

- Use this document to dynamically assemble teams for ARAS microservice deployments in any project.
- Assign tasks and responsibilities based on the defined roles.
- Ensure cross-team collaboration and regular feedback loops.
- Adapt team composition as needed for project scale, domain focus, or technical requirements.
- Leverage domain experts to validate data, scenarios, and analytical models.
- Use technical experts to guarantee system robustness, scalability, and security.
